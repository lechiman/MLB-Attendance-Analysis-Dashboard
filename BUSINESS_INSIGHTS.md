# MLB Attendance Analysis - Business Insights Documentation

## Executive Summary

This project provides a comprehensive analytics dashboard for analyzing MLB game attendance data from 2000-2025 (26 seasons), offering valuable business insights for team management, marketing professionals, sports economists, and MLB executives. The analysis reveals critical patterns in fan engagement, revenue optimization opportunities, and strategic decision-making metrics, including the significant 2025 stabilization after years of decline.

## Key Business Questions Answered

### 1. **Return on Investment (ROI) Analysis**
- **Question**: "Is our payroll investment translating into fan attendance?"
- **Insight**: The dashboard provides payroll vs. attendance correlation analysis, showing the relationship between team spending and fan draw. **NEW: Championship analysis shows winning provides +31% attendance premium (+613K fans/season) and +34% post-championship bump**
- **Business Impact**: Helps justify or adjust payroll budgets based on actual attendance impact. Championships deliver $150M+ in additional revenue over 5 years
- **Metric**: Attendance Efficiency (Attendance per Million $ of Payroll), Championship Premium (+613K fans)

### 2. **Competitive Positioning**
- **Question**: "How does our team's attendance compare to league competitors?"
- **Insight**: Season-specific rankings showing top performers and market share
- **Business Impact**: Identifies market position and competitive gaps
- **Metric**: Ranking by total attendance and attendance per game

### 3. **Long-term Trend Analysis**
- **Question**: "Are we gaining or losing fan base over time?"
- **Insight**: Multi-year trend analysis showing growth or decline patterns
- **Business Impact**: Early warning system for fan base erosion or validation of growth strategies
- **Metric**: Year-over-year percentage changes in attendance

### 4. **League-Wide Market Health**
- **Question**: "Is baseball growing or declining as an entertainment product?"
- **Insight**: Aggregate league attendance trends over 26 years (2000-2025), with 2025 showing stabilization at 71.4M fans (+0.09%)
- **Business Impact**: Informs investment decisions and long-term strategic planning; 2025 data suggests potential turning point
- **Metric**: Total league attendance and average attendance per game
